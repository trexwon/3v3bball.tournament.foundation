HOW CAN YOU GET INVOLVED?

Be on the lookout for posters around the school and on the AES social medias. Simply participate in the event with friends, join with a team and pay the entry fee, and/or donate to our cause through donation boxes. For any further inquiries, contact us through our organization email: eduthroughbasketball@gmail.com

HOW CAN YOU HELP?

We will be setting up 7 different donation boxes for funds around the school and at the event, which accept any donation made by other students, parents, or faculty. Spread the word around to friends or family members and encourage them to attend or participate in the fundraiser. Create social media posts to spread awareness of the event.
