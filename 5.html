Introduce ourselves

From left: Tristan, Lee, Jihong, Joshua

We are a group of 9th grade students in AES. Our project is to raise funds through a 3v3 basketball event and, together with an NGO called Palna(which supports children in need of shelter, family, and basic life necessities), raise awareness and educate malnourished kids in the camps near AES, such as Vivekanand and Sanjay.
