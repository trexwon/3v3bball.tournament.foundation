Teaching young kids about nutrition is imperative to keeping their bodies healthy and strong. Unfortunately, a lot of schools and community groups in impoverished areas do not have enough resources to run nutrition programs, meaning kids are often eating a non-balanced diet, and only consuming what is readily available(usually junk food like chips). Since basketball is a fun sport that many people here at AES like to watch and play, if we are able to run a basketball tournament, we can use the money from people buying tickets and making donations to create classes and activities about nutrition for kids.

Within these programs, kids will learn about all the different types of healthy foods, the importance of a balanced diet, and how to pick the right things to eat. Helping kids learn about nutrition when they're young will hopefully make them eat healthier for the rest of their lives. Raising money through a sports event to teach nutrition can be an awesome way to improve the health of students and other communities, especially young people.
