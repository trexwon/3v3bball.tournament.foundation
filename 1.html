Our group's goal is to educate people in camps about health and nutrition. The proceeds from the entry fees and donations will go towards creating a program where children in Vivekanand and Sanjay camps can learn about a healthy diet. They will learn why a balanced diet is important, what different foods do for your body, and how to make good food choices. By running this event, we hope to not only raise the funds we need to educate kids about nutrition, but also help them develop long-term healthy habits for life. The basketball tournament will be fun for all participants in our community at aes and get students involved in supporting the well-being of local youth.
